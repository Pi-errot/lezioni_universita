# Conditionals
## If conditional (pag 140)

> if + past simple, subject + would + base form
- Ex: If you studied, you would pass your exams 
- Tradotto: Se studiassi, passeresti l'esami

## Second conditional
> if + past simple, subject + would + infinite form
### Quando dai un consiglio ad una persona specifica
- Ex: If i were you, i would study more

### Quando dai un idea
- Ex: if i was an astronaut, i would travel through space.

## Third conditional
> If + past perfect, subject + would have + past participle.
- Ex: If Mark had studied, he would have passed his exam

---

1. if he loved you, he would ask you to marry him.
2. if he loved you, he would have asked you to marry him.
3. if he had loved you, he would have asked you to marry him.
